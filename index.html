Bruno Castanho Silva

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Cologne Center for Comparative Politics, University of Cologne. I have earned my PhD in Political Science from Central European University in Budapest, and my research interests are on quantitative methods, more specifically causal inference, machine learning, and structural equation modeling. I've also got a paper or two (or ten...) on populism.

You can reach me by email at bcsilva@wiso.uni-koeln.de, or on Twitter at @b_castanho.
